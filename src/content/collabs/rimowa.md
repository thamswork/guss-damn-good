intro: When K. Mapprang and K. Ton approached us to design a signature flavor
  for their wedding celebration, we wanted to capture the essence of a timeless
  romance — something elegant, light, and unforgettable for their guests.
challenge: The couple requested a flavor that felt both celebratory and
  refreshing. It needed to reflect their shared love for travel and summer
  sunsets, blending floral notes with a touch of classic sparkling luxury.
process: We spent several sessions refining the balance between the delicate
  sweetness of Japanese white peach and the crisp acidity of premium Rose
  Champagne. After five iterations, we perfected a smooth, fragrant sorbet that
  cleanses the palate and sparkles on the tongue.
result: Served as the grand finale dessert for 300 guests at their wedding
  reception. The flavor was a hit, perfectly complementing the floral theme and
  providing a sophisticated, refreshing end to a beautiful evening.
